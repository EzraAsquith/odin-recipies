Slow Cooker Pepper Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs beef (such as sirloin, flank, or round steak), cut into strips
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • ¼ cup light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 tsp crushed garlic
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 can (14.1 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • ¼ cup boiling water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp water

Instructions:

  1. Brown the Beef: In a frypan over high heat, quickly brown the beef strips.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, combine the boiling water and the beef bouillon cube. Stir until dissolved. Add the soy sauce, ginger, garlic, black pepper, crushed tomatoes, and brown sugar. Mix well.
  3. Combine Ingredients: Place the browned beef, bell peppers, and onion in the slow cooker. Pour the sauce over the top.
  4. Cook: Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the beef is tender.
  5. Thicken the Sauce: About 30 minutes before serving, mix the cornstarch and water in a small bowl. Stir into the slow cooker to thicken the sauce.
  6. Serve: Serve over rice, noodles, or mashed potatoes.
